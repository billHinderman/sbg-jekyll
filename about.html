About Starbase Go.

Starbase Go is a design studio. We create magnetic digital work that distills information and elicits action.

We build websites, apps, visualizations, and brand strategies. We believe that focus and cleanliness in design and message leads to more rewarding experiences.

We were born in Chicago, originally as the collaboration of two front-end developers wanting to push the boundaries of digital design, with specific focus on: responsive web design, data visualization, and spotless API development.

About our director.

Bill Hinderman is the creative director and front-end development lead at Starbase Go. He's also the author of a book on the processes of building code-driven data visualization upon the principles of responsive web design.
